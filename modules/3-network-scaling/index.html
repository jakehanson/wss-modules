Instructions

To add a node to the network, click the "Add Node" button, and then click where you'd like to place the node.

To connect two nodes, click the "Add Edge" button, and then click on each node you'd like to connect to each other.

To remove a node, click "Remove Node" and then click on the node you wish to remove.

To remove an edge, click "Remove Edge", and then click the two nodes connected by the edge you wish to remove.
