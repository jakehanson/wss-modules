Elementary Cellular Automata

Instructions:

You can click the cells in the bottom-most row of the array to toggle their state. You can choose the number of cells, the speed of the simulation, and the ECA rule.

Once you have chosen your settings, you can toggle the state and run a simulation of the model using the other controls:

Step
Take one step in the simulation
Start
Run the simulation
Stop
Stop the simulation
Restart
Stop and clear the simulation
