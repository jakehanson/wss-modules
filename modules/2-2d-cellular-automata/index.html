Game of Life

Can we simulate life?

Can we simulate life? Or something that looks like life?

Let's play a game, a game of life. Consider a 2D world, where lifes (or agents, cells, etc.) live on a lattice. Each cell can be alive or dead, represented by black or white. All cells follow the same rule: They can only live in certain density enviroments. If it is too crowded or sparse, they will die. If the density is proper, a live (black) cell can emerge on a dead (white) cell.

size:

Rule of Game of Life

  • Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbors dies, as if by underpopulation.
  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbors lives on to the next generation.
  • Any live cell with more than three live neighbors dies, as if by overpopulation.
  • Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbors becomes a live cell, as if by reproduction.

Instruction for the simulator

This is a simulator for the "Game of Life". After setting initial pattern by clicking "Set random cells" or by licking cells, click "run" button, and you will see how the system evoloves. "step" button will evaluate the system for one step, and "run" will do it repeatedly.
